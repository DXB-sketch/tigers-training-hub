Technical — Pressing & Recovery
High Press Trigger: Shape & Recover
Coordinated front-foot pressure on GK distribution, with structured recovery shape on turnover
Red: defending / pressing team
Blue: attacking / building team
Player run
Pass option
Description
  • Red holds a high defensive block in the attacking half. Blue build from GK distribution.
  • Press triggered the moment the ball reaches the centre back. Nearest red striker leads at an angle, cutting off the square pass.
  • Blue must play through pressure using quick combinations. Full backs provide short outlet; striker drops as forward option.
  • On winning possession red transitions immediately into a 4-2 recovery shape, both CMs dropping to cover.
Setup
  • Mark a 30 x 40 m zone in one half.
  • Place 2 medium goals, one at each end.
  • 6 spare balls behind each goal for quick restarts.
  • Red team in bibs. Blue team in contrasting bibs.
  • GK starts with ball every rep. Red team sets pressing shape before each restart.
Organisation
Area
30 x 40 m
Goals
2 x medium
Balls
6 spare
Rest
90 sec between reps
Progressions
  • Easier: allow GK to play long ball, removing the press decision.
  • Standard: press on CB touch as shown.
  • Harder: reduce red to 4 players, forcing tighter pressing angles.
  • Game condition: add a target striker for blue. Creates counter-press on turnover.
Coaching points
  • Press leader (ST): angle to cut off the square ball, not straight at the GK.
  • CMs: follow immediately on trigger. No ball-watching.
  • GK: call "press!" to trigger; "hold!" to delay.
  • Blue FBs: first touch away from pressure, always facing forward.
